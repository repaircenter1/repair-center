How to Ship for Repair

1

Contact Us First

Reach out via text, Telegram, or email to discuss your repair needs, timeline, and get a preliminary cost estimate before shipping.

2

Package Securely

Pack your device with extra protection so it can safely return in the same packaging. Use anti-static bags for electronics and adequate padding. You are responsible for secure packaging.

3

Choose a Carrier

Select a carrier and after shipping, send us the tracking number, package details, brief problem description, and your contact information for return delivery.

4

We'll Handle the Rest

After free diagnostics, we'll contact you to confirm repair costs and timeline. Once complete, the device passes testing, and after payment, we ship it back to you.

Repair Terms & Conditions

⚡ Free Diagnostics

We provide free diagnostics for all devices within 3 days of receipt. Free diagnostics do not include soldering or any paid services. All paid services are agreed upon in advance. You'll receive a detailed report and repair quote before any work begins.

⏱️ Repair Timeline

Standard diagnostics completed within 3 days. Repair work typically takes additional time depending on parts availability and complexity.

💰 Payment Terms

Payment is due after repair completion. We accept cash, e-transfer, and other electronic payment methods. No surprise charges – you approve the quote first.

🛡️ Warranty

All repairs come with a 14-day warranty on parts and labor. If the same issue that the device originally came in with reoccurs within this period, we'll fix it at no charge.

❌ Unrepairable Devices

If repair is not possible or cost-effective, we'll notify you immediately. Your device will be returned at no charge except for return shipping.

✅ Our Commitment

We treat every device with professional care. However, electronics repair carries inherent risks. We use industry-standard practices and stand behind our work.
